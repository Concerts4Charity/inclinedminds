About the Film

This story reads like a time capsule with an archival bookmark (on) in the city of Asheville. A glimpse into past experiences with an evolving creative community uncovers a wealth of talented people, but this collection of voices is a finite sampling. A pilot idea that can be rehashed in perpetuity, this city is an incubation stepping stone, one that embraces the natural life cycle of talent infusion and departure (diffusion). These individuals have the drive to contribute and inspire, balancing secondary endeavors with their everyday commitments. A healthy conversation continues to develop between creatives, activists and entrepreneurs. Artistic and philanthropic support centers act as a springboard, providing clear paths for collaborative opportunities. There will always be influential people migrating in and out of the region and our continued goal is to highlight intersections between artistic expression and goodwill. Creative hospitality is an overriding fixture of Western North Carolina and we want to reveal how those instances of cross-pollination provide a better quality of life.
