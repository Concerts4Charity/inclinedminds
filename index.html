How Creativity Can Be A Source For Social Change

This story reads like a time capsule with an archival bookmark on the city of Asheville. A pilot idea that can be rehashed in perpetuity, this region is an incubation stepping stone, one that embraces healthy conversation between creatives, activists and entrepreneurs. The evolving community is using centralized artistic and philanthropic support centers to not only provide mentorship, but also offer clear paths for collaborative opportunities. Our continued goal is to highlight intersections between innovation and social outreach, hoping to measure that impact and develop programatic solutions for underserved community needs.
